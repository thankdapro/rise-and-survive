What is Rise & Survive?

A progressive apocalypse that escalates every single day

Rise and Survive transforms your Minecraft Bedrock world into a haunting wasteland where zombies don't just spawn — they evolve. The dynamic difficulty bar increases every day, ramping spawn rates, speed, and abilities. Every 8 days, the horde unlocks something new and brutal.

Difficulty Climbs Every Day

The difficulty bar nudges up with every in-game day you survive. Spawn rates increase, zombies grow faster and stronger, and new variants slowly unlock. There's no plateau — the world keeps getting harder for as long as you stay alive.

Daily · Permanent

And Every So Often… A Blood Moon Rises

Without warning, the sky turns red and the horde pours in. Blood Moons are massive, server-shaking spawn events that drop a relentless wave of zombies at your doorstep until sunrise. If your walls are weak, this is when they fall.

Core Features

Why this addon hits different

Built on top of vanilla Bedrock without breaking the survival loop you already love.

Dynamic Difficulty

A live difficulty meter increases every in-game day, boosting spawn rates and unlocking new zombie behaviors as you survive longer.

Equipment Evolution

Higher difficulty means a higher chance zombies spawn already wearing armor or carrying weapons — including iron and diamond gear at the highest tiers.

7+ Zombie Variants

Miners, Block Placers, Witches, Bombers, Enders, Jumpers, Crawlers — each with unique behaviors that punish lazy base designs.

Wasteland Worldgen

Pair with Ranzie's Overgrown Cities (ROC) for abandoned cities, ruined buildings, and immersive new biomes filled with loot and danger.

Endless Hordes

No matter how high your walls, no matter how far underground — the horde finds a way. Towering, bridging, teleporting, digging.

Vanilla-Compatible

Drops into existing or new worlds without rewriting biomes. Toggle the experimental "Beta APIs" flag in world settings and you're good to go.

Meet the Horde

The zombies that will end your run

Each variant unlocks at a different difficulty tier. Plan your defenses accordingly.

Miner Zombie

Breaks blocks to reach you. Speeds up dramatically on higher difficulties — your wall is on a timer.

Block Placer

Towers up, bridges across gaps, and chases you no matter how high you build. Skybases are not safe.

Witch Zombie

Throws and drinks potions — poison, weakness, slowness. Resistant to physical damage when buffed up.

Bomber Zombie

Throws TNT (damage-only — your blocks survive). Forces you out of cover and into the open.

Ender Zombie

Teleports using ender pearls. Walls don't matter. Distance doesn't matter. Stay aware of your surroundings.

Jumper Zombie

Leaps at you and onto blocks. A two-block-high wall isn't enough — they'll launch right over.

Crawler Zombie

Crawls through 1-block-high gaps. Your front door's gap under it? That's their door now.

Geared Zombie

End-game spawn. Wears full armor and swings iron or diamond weapons. One hit can shred unprepared players.

How to Download & Install

From .mcaddon to apocalypse in 2 minutes

Always grab Rise and Survive from the official CurseForge page. Avoid third-party mirrors — outdated or modified files have been reported.

1

Download .mcaddon

On the CurseForge page, open the Files tab and download the latest Ranzie Rise and Survive 1.2 latest.mcaddon.

2

Open the file

On phone, tap the file — Minecraft Bedrock auto-imports both the behavior and resource pack. On PC, double-click to open with Minecraft.

3

Apply to a world

Create or edit a world. Under Behavior Packs and Resource Packs, activate "Rise and Survive." Enable Beta APIs in Experiments.

4

Spawn & survive

Spawn in and start fortifying immediately — the horde scales from your very first night. There is no easing-in period, and the difficulty bar never stops climbing.

FAQ

Common survivor questions

Is Rise and Survive really free?

Yes. The addon is published free on CurseForge and MCPEDL. If a website is asking you to pay or fill out a survey to download it, it's not the official source.

Does it work on Java Edition?

No — this is a Bedrock-only addon (.mcaddon). It works on Bedrock across Windows 10/11, iOS, Android, Xbox, PlayStation, and Nintendo Switch as long as you can import addons on your platform.

Why are achievements disabled?

The addon requires the Beta APIs experiment, and Mojang automatically disables achievements on any world with experimental features turned on. This is a Minecraft limitation, not the addon's fault.

Can I use it on a Realm or with friends?

Yes. Apply the resource and behavior packs to your Realm or world, and anyone joining will download them automatically. Good luck — squad survival is intense.

Do these zombies replace vanilla zombies?

Rise and Survive layers progressive variants on top of the vanilla zombie. Standard zombies still spawn early on; specialized variants unlock as the difficulty bar climbs.

Where do I find the Discord invite?

The active Discord invite is posted on Ranzie's official CurseForge and Patreon pages — that way the link stays fresh whenever the server updates. Use the Discord button on this page to head straight there.

I imported the addon but nothing changed.

Three usual culprits: (1) Behavior pack not activated for the world, (2) Beta APIs experiment not enabled, (3) you're still on Day 1 — variants unlock with progression. Check the world settings first.
